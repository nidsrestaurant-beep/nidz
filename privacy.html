Home / Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Nid'Z Restaurant ("we", "us") collects and uses information when you use this website or place an order.

Information We Collect

When you submit a contact form or online order, we collect the details you provide — such as your name, phone number, email address, and message content — solely to respond to your request.

Payments

Card payments made through our "Order Online" page for pickup are processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your card details on our servers — Stripe handles all payment data in line with its own security and privacy practices. Delivery orders are placed and paid for through Wolt, which handles that data under its own privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

This site links to and embeds services including Google Maps, Google Reviews, Instagram, TikTok, TripAdvisor and Wolt. These third parties may collect information according to their own privacy policies when you interact with their embedded content or links.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, reach us at nidsrestaurant@gmail.com or +995 571 530 290.
